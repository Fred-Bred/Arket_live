Regressionsligningen i dette tilfælde:
$$Y ̂=bX+a=0.56 \times X+2.1$$

Standard error of $b$:
$$s_b=\frac{s_{Y-\hat{Y}}}{(s_X \sqrt{N-1)}}=\frac{0.58}{(1.64\sqrt{6-1}}=0.157$$
